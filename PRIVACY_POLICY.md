# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2, 2026**

## Overview
Mirror is a minimalist mood tracking application that collects mood check-ins and optional email addresses for insights.

## Data Collection
- **Mood Data**: Your mood selections are stored locally and on our servers with timestamps
- **Email Addresses**: If you provide an email, it is stored with your mood data to enable "The Veil" premium feature
- **Device Storage**: Check-in counts are stored in your device's local storage

## Data Usage
- Mood data is used to generate personalized philosophical insights
- Email addresses are used to send monthly personalized insights (The Veil feature)
- We do not share your data with third parties

## Data Storage
- All data is stored securely on our backend servers
- Local device data is stored in your browser's localStorage

## Your Rights
- You can request deletion of your data at any time
- You can export your mood history
- You can opt out of The Veil emails at any time

## Contact
For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@mirrorapp.com

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Continued use of Mirror constitutes acceptance of any changes.
